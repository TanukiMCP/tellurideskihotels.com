## Group Capacity Analysis

Different hotels accommodate different group sizes effectively, with properties offering various room configurations and meeting facilities that match group needs.

**Small Groups (6-10 people):** Most hotels accommodate small groups through suites, connecting rooms, or multiple standard rooms. Properties like Hotel Telluride and The Madeline provide good options with meeting spaces for small group activities.

**Mid-Size Groups (10-20 people):** Properties with meeting facilities and multiple room configurations work best. The Madeline Hotel and Lumiere Telluride provide meeting spaces and accommodations that accommodate mid-size groups effectively.

**Large Groups (20+ people):** Properties with extensive meeting facilities and multiple accommodation options are essential. The Peaks Resort provides the most comprehensive facilities for large groups, with meeting spaces accommodating up to 500 people and multiple accommodation configurations.

## Space Options for Groups

Different hotels offer various space configurations that accommodate groups differently, from suites and connecting rooms to condominium units with multiple bedrooms.

**Suites and Connecting Rooms:** Properties like Hotel Telluride and The Madeline offer suites and connecting rooms that accommodate groups while maintaining individual privacy. This approach works well for groups wanting hotel services with group connection.

**Condominium Units:** Properties like Lumiere Telluride and Fairmont Heritage Place offer condominium units with multiple bedrooms, kitchens, and common areas. This approach provides space advantages for larger groups wanting residential-style accommodations.

**Multiple Standard Rooms:** Most hotels accommodate groups through multiple standard rooms, though this approach may reduce group cohesion compared to suites or condominium units. Group coordination services help maintain group connection.

## Group Services Comparison

Group services vary significantly across properties, with some offering comprehensive coordination while others provide basic group booking support.

**Comprehensive Services:** The Peaks Resort provides dedicated group coordinators, event planning services, and comprehensive support for group logistics. This level of service simplifies group planning significantly.

**Standard Services:** Most properties offer group booking support, meeting space coordination, and basic group services. Properties like The Madeline provide good group support without comprehensive event planning.

**Basic Services:** Some properties provide group booking support without extensive coordination services. Groups may need to handle more logistics independently.
