## Value Analysis by Group Size

Value rankings shift based on group size, as per-person costs and included amenities affect value differently for different group sizes.

**2-Person Groups:** Downtown hotels like Hotel Telluride and The New Sheridan provide best value, offering reasonable rates, included amenities, and excellent locations. Budget options like Oak Street Inn provide significant savings while maintaining good locations.

**4-Person Groups:** Downtown hotels continue providing best value, with condominium options like Lumiere becoming competitive when kitchen facilities reduce dining costs. Mountain Village properties provide value through convenience despite higher rates.

**6-Person Groups:** Condominium options provide better value due to space advantages and kitchen facilities. Hotels require multiple rooms, increasing per-person costs, while condos accommodate groups more efficiently.

## Value Optimization Tips

**Book Early:** Advance bookings (60+ days) provide 10-20% savings regardless of property type.

**Consider Included Amenities:** Properties with included breakfast, equipment storage, or parking reduce other costs, improving overall value.

**Evaluate Location Value:** Downtown properties provide gondola access and walkability, while Mountain Village properties provide ski-in/ski-out convenience. Choose based on priorities.

**Factor in Dining Savings:** Properties with kitchen facilities reduce dining costs by 40-50%, significantly improving value for longer stays or larger groups.

**Compare Total Costs:** Look beyond room rates to total trip costs, including dining, activities, and transportation savings from location advantages.
