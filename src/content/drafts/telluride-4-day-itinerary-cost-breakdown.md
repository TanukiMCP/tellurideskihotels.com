## Day 1: Arrival and Orientation

**Morning:** Arrive in Telluride, check into accommodations, and pick up rental equipment. Allow 2-3 hours for check-in, equipment pickup, and settling in.

**Afternoon:** Take the free gondola ride between downtown Telluride and Mountain Village for orientation and scenic views. This provides excellent introduction to the area while costing nothing.

**Evening:** Group dinner at a casual restaurant. Explore downtown Telluride or Mountain Village depending on your accommodation location.

**Cost Breakdown by Group Size:**

- **2 People:** Accommodations $300-800/night ($150-400 pp), Equipment rental $100-160 ($50-80 pp), Dinner $60-100 ($30-50 pp) = **$260-640 per person**
- **4 People:** Accommodations $400-900/night ($100-225 pp), Equipment rental $100-160 ($50-80 pp), Dinner $120-200 ($30-50 pp) = **$180-355 per person**
- **6 People:** Accommodations $600-1,200/night ($100-200 pp), Equipment rental $100-160 ($50-80 pp), Dinner $180-300 ($30-50 pp) = **$180-330 per person**
- **8 People:** Accommodations $800-1,600/night ($100-200 pp), Equipment rental $100-160 ($50-80 pp), Dinner $240-400 ($30-50 pp) = **$180-330 per person**

## Day 2: Full Day of Skiing

**Morning:** Early start for first tracks (9:00 AM). Ski until lunch, focusing on terrain appropriate for your group's ability levels.

**Lunch:** On-mountain dining or packed lunch if staying in accommodations with kitchen facilities.

**Afternoon:** Continue skiing, exploring different areas of the mountain. Take breaks as needed for different ability levels.

**Evening:** Après-ski at a Mountain Village or downtown spot, followed by casual group dinner.

**Cost Breakdown by Group Size:**

- **2 People:** Lift tickets $400 ($200 pp), Lunch $40-80 ($20-40 pp), Après-ski $30-60 ($15-30 pp), Dinner $60-100 ($30-50 pp) = **$265-390 per person**
- **4 People:** Lift tickets $400 ($200 pp), Lunch $80-160 ($20-40 pp), Après-ski $60-120 ($15-30 pp), Dinner $120-200 ($30-50 pp) = **$265-390 per person**
- **6 People:** Lift tickets $400 ($200 pp), Lunch $120-240 ($20-40 pp), Après-ski $90-180 ($15-30 pp), Dinner $180-300 ($30-50 pp) = **$265-390 per person**
- **8 People:** Lift tickets $400 ($200 pp), Lunch $160-320 ($20-40 pp), Après-ski $120-240 ($15-30 pp), Dinner $240-400 ($30-50 pp) = **$265-390 per person**

*Note: Groups staying in condos can reduce lunch and dinner costs by 40-50% through group cooking.*

## Day 3: Skiing and Activities

**Morning:** Skiing with optional group lesson for beginners or those wanting to improve skills.

**Afternoon:** Continue skiing or try non-skiing activities like snowshoeing, ice skating, or exploring town.

**Evening:** Group dinner at a mid-range restaurant, followed by optional evening activities.

**Cost Breakdown by Group Size:**

- **2 People:** Lift tickets $400 ($200 pp), Optional lesson $180-400 ($90-200 pp), Activities $0-100 ($0-50 pp), Dinner $80-150 ($40-75 pp) = **$230-525 per person**
- **4 People:** Lift tickets $400 ($200 pp), Optional lesson $180-400 ($45-100 pp), Activities $0-200 ($0-50 pp), Dinner $160-300 ($40-75 pp) = **$205-375 per person**
- **6 People:** Lift tickets $400 ($200 pp), Optional lesson $180-400 ($30-67 pp), Activities $0-300 ($0-50 pp), Dinner $240-450 ($40-75 pp) = **$200-362 per person**
- **8 People:** Lift tickets $400 ($200 pp), Optional lesson $180-400 ($23-50 pp), Activities $0-400 ($0-50 pp), Dinner $320-600 ($40-75 pp) = **$198-360 per person**

*Note: Group lessons provide better per-person value for larger groups. Activities are optional and can be adjusted based on preferences.*

## Day 4: Final Skiing and Departure

**Morning:** Final skiing session, focusing on favorite runs or areas not yet explored.

**Afternoon:** Check out of accommodations, return equipment, and depart. Some groups extend with lunch before departure.

**Evening:** Departure (or optional final group meal for groups staying later).

**Cost Breakdown by Group Size:**

- **2 People:** Lift tickets $400 ($200 pp), Optional lunch $30-50 ($15-25 pp) = **$215-225 per person**
- **4 People:** Lift tickets $400 ($200 pp), Optional lunch $60-100 ($15-25 pp) = **$215-225 per person**
- **6 People:** Lift tickets $400 ($200 pp), Optional lunch $90-150 ($15-25 pp) = **$215-225 per person**
- **8 People:** Lift tickets $400 ($200 pp), Optional lunch $120-200 ($15-25 pp) = **$215-225 per person**

*Note: Final day costs are lower as accommodations are typically checked out in the morning. Groups can adjust departure times based on travel schedules.*

## Cost Optimization Tips by Group Size

**2-Person Groups:** Optimize by choosing mid-range accommodations, booking multi-day lift tickets in advance, balancing fine dining with casual meals, and choosing activities that provide good value. Consider condos with kitchen facilities if planning to cook some meals.

**4-Person Groups:** Optimize by choosing condos with kitchen facilities that reduce dining costs, coordinating lift ticket purchases, booking group activities when possible, and comparing accommodation options for best per-person value. Group cooking can significantly reduce dining expenses.

**6-Person Groups:** Optimize by choosing vacation rentals that accommodate the group efficiently, coordinating group bookings to access discounts, utilizing kitchen facilities extensively, and coordinating group activities. Group coordination provides access to discounts and cost-sharing benefits.

**8-Person Groups:** Optimize by choosing large vacation rentals that provide best per-person value, coordinating group bookings to access maximum discounts, utilizing kitchen facilities extensively, and coordinating group activities. Large groups benefit most from cost-sharing and group discounts.
