## Content Type Guidelines

### Pillar Content (3,000+ words)
- Comprehensive topic coverage
- Multiple H2 sections (8-12)
- 10-15 internal links
- Rich media (images, tables, maps)
- FAQ section required
- Updated quarterly

### Comprehensive Guides (2,500-2,999 words)
- Deep dive on specific topic
- 6-8 H2 sections
- 8-10 internal links
- Practical tips and advice
- FAQ section recommended

### Listicles (2,000-2,499 words)
- Numbered or ranked items
- 10-20 items typical
- Brief descriptions (100-150 words each)
- Quick-scan format
- Comparison tables when applicable

### Comparisons (1,800-2,499 words)
- Head-to-head analysis
- Comparison table required
- Pros/cons for each option
- Clear recommendation
- Decision framework

### Seasonal/Timely (1,500-2,000 words)
- Event or season-specific
- Time-sensitive information
- Updated annually
- Promotional opportunities
- Booking CTAs prominent
