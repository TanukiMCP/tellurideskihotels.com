## Keyword Usage Guidelines

### Primary Keyword Placement
1. Title (H1) - Include near beginning
2. Meta title - Within first 40 characters
3. Meta description - Natural inclusion
4. First 100 words of content
5. At least one H2 subheading
6. URL slug
7. Image alt text (hero image)
8. Naturally throughout (0.5-1.5% density)

### Secondary Keywords (3-5 per article)
- H2/H3 subheadings
- Section introductions
- Image alt text
- Internal link anchor text
- Naturally distributed

### LSI Keywords (10-15 per article)
- Natural variations
- Related terms
- Synonyms
- Context-appropriate usage
